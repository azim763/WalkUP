About WalkUp!

Creating a community through outdoor adventures.

Our team of passionate adventure enthusiasts has come together to create a platform for all your outdoor adventures.

The great outdoors is where memories are made, and our goal is to help you make them in the most exciting way. Whether you're an experienced adventurer or just starting out, our app has everything you need to plan and execute your adventure. From hiking to camping, rock climbing to kayaking, our app provides options to all your favorite outdoor activities. The newest feature offers checklists, advice, equipment recommendations and gear center to ensure that your adventure is safe, enjoyable, and unforgettable. At our core, we believe that there is nothing more rewarding than exploring the world around us, and our app is dedicated to making that exploration as accessible and enjoyable as possible. So, come join us on the journey, and let's explore the great outdoors together!
